Spatial Awareness

About the Game

A simon spin-off game that can help train your spatial awareness.

The Rules

In the spatial awareness game, a sector of the board will flash quickly and the goal is to click the sector that flashed. There are no borders in the game, so you have to be very accurate.

The Game Is Over.

Thanks for playing this game and supporting developers in training. If you'd like to play again, click the "Start" button on the main screen.
